About Us

This game is a grand strategy game that involves warfare. Economic. Industry. Diplomacy and more. Here you will have control of the government. There is no goal to this story. It is for you to craft and make as you see fit. Make a story for the future generations to talk about. Advance your country to the number 1 in the world.

Economics

In this game you can advance your country's economic, make policies and decisions to advance your country's Economic base, increase your citizen's standard of living so that they can enjoy their livelihoods.

Industry

This century involves the Industrial Revolution. It is your choice to get your country to Industrialise and become the Industrial superpower in the world. Outproduce your enemies, no one can stop you.

Diplomacy

Manage the diplomatic situation in your country and abroad, be aware of enemies, and make allies. But also beware of your allies's interest as you can be prone to backstab. Be aware of both allies and enemies.

Colonisation

Colonise the world! Expand your empires, expand your Industry and expand your influence, one of the best ways to secure resources and prestige in this time of the world!

Fast Changing World

The world is changing more than ever! There is none who can oppose. But one that can lead in these fast times. The only thing that remains. Will you lead this country at the forefront?

What Are You Waiting For?

A world that is waiting to be interrupted is in your fingertips. Forge your own story and your own tails, this time of history is yours to influence.
